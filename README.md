# 🎲 Sudoku-Android

Welcome to Sudoku-Android, where solving puzzles meets mobile gaming! Dive into the world of Sudoku with this simple yet feature-packed Android game.

## Features 🌟

- **Draft Mode** 📝: Jot down your thoughts without committing to a move.
- **Auto Solve** 🤖: Feeling stuck? Let the app lend you a hand.
- **Leaderboard** 🏆: Compete with friends and become a Sudoku maestro.
- **Undo** ↩️: A mistake? No worries, take a step back.
